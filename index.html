Hidden layer revealed

Guardian Sight

AR that serves the place.

Guardian Sight is the optional augmented reality system inside Unlocking Bulgaria. It reveals hidden layers, symbols, artifacts, reconstructed moments, and route clues without making AR mandatory.

Accessibility comes first. Every AR moment has a non-AR path with readable content, captions, and clear completion logic.

Regions of Bulgaria

Every region becomes a domain.

Regions are presented as collectible domains with their own routes, relics, visual identity, progress model, and final seal.

Danube and Northwest

Domain of Stone and River

Fortresses, rivers, Roman roads, and Belogradchik stone formations.

Sofia and Shopluk

Domain of Crossroads

Urban layers, Serdica ruins, Vitosha paths, springs, and markets.

Thracian Valley

Domain of Kings

Tombs, ritual hills, roses, wine country, and golden artifacts.

Rhodope Mountains

Domain of Voice and Stone

Orpheus myths, caves, bridges, villages, forests, and music.

Black Sea Coast

Domain of the Sea Gate

Ancient ports, capes, islands, salt lakes, and sea monasteries.

Old Capitals

Domain of the Name

Pliska, Preslav, Tarnovo, manuscripts, crowns, walls, and statehood.

FAQ

Questions before the journey.

Is Unlocking Bulgaria a game?

No. It uses game-like progress, relics, and rewards, but the real product is cultural exploration in real places.

Do I need AR to complete a route?

No. Guardian Sight is optional. Every AR moment has an accessible alternative.

Who is it for?

Bulgarian travelers, families, history lovers, schools, foreign tourists, municipalities, museums, and tourism organizations.

Can partners create routes?

Yes. Municipalities, museums, schools, and tourism businesses can collaborate on route design, cultural validation, and visitor engagement.

Will routes work offline?

The product should support offline preparation for route content where connectivity is limited.
